About

I am a PhD Candidate in Sociology and Social Research at the University of Trento. My research focuses on secularization and ideological bias in science. I primarily work with primary survey data and survey experiments, utilizing quantitative methods and computational text analysis. I was a visiting PhD student at the University of Lausanne and a Teaching Assistant for courses on Public Opinion Research and Research Design.
